Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We’re open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM including weekends and holidays.

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes! We have a selection of plant-based milks, vegan pastries, and gluten-free desserts available.

Is Wi-Fi available for customers?

Absolutely! Free Wi-Fi is available for all our customers. Just ask our staff for the password.

Can I reserve a table in advance?

Yes, please contact us via our Contact Page to make a reservation or call us directly.

Do you host private events?

We do! Reach out to us for custom event packages, including catering and space rental options.
