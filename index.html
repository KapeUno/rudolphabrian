Welcome to 私-Kape-Uno

At 私-Kape-Uno, we serve the finest hand-crafted coffee, expertly brewed from the best beans around the world. Whether you’re in the mood for a creamy cappuccino, a rich espresso, or a chilled iced brew, we’ve got you covered. Pair it with one of our freshly baked pastries for the perfect experience. Step inside and enjoy the cozy ambiance where every cup tells a story.

Our Featured Brews

Whether you prefer your coffee hot or cold, we’ve handpicked a selection of our most popular and exquisite brews. Each cup is carefully prepared to perfection. Here are a few of our signature offerings:

The Classic Latte

A smooth espresso combined with steamed milk and a light foam top. Perfectly balanced.

The Rich Cappuccino

Espresso with a velvety foam, delivering a rich and creamy coffee experience.

Iced Coffee

Chilled brewed coffee served with ice and a touch of sweetness. The perfect refresher on a warm day.

Freshly Baked Pastries

No coffee is complete without a delicious pastry on the side! Our bakery offers an array of freshly baked goods that pair perfectly with your favorite brew. From buttery croissants to moist muffins, each pastry is made with love and care.

Classic Butter Croissant

Flaky, golden-brown croissant with a rich, buttery flavor. A perfect morning treat.

Blueberry Muffin

Soft, moist muffin bursting with fresh blueberries. Ideal with a cup of coffee or tea.
